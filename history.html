The History of Pogs!!!

Pogs has two possible origin stories and was originally called "Milk Caps".
One starts in Hawaii in the 1920's. The other is Japan. In Hawaii, the game
was played using milk bottle caps that were rendered obsolete when new
packaging came out for containing milk.
